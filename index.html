FR!ZZ's GAME RULES:

This game has two boxes, one named the ROUND-SCORE(The white box) while the other is named GLOBAL-SCORE(The black box with red borders.). The round score is where the dice or numbers you roll goes into while the Global-Score is where the saved "Round-scores" goes into.

The ROLL button is the button for rolling a dice.

The HOLD button is the button for adding your Round-scores to your Global-Score. Note: Whenever you use this, you lose your turn and it's the next player's turn to roll dice.

The NEW GAME button is used to reset the game and start a new one.

Fill the input below to choose the total score you would prefer a winner to be declared.

1. This game has two players, playing in rounds.

2. In each turn, a player rolls a dice as many times as he/she wishes. Each result gets added to his/her "Round-Score".

3. BUT, if the player rolls a one(1), you lose your "Round-score", and after that, it's the second player's turn.

4. But you can choose to "HOLD", by pressing the hold button so as to save the numbers previously rolled in your round to your GLOBAL SCORE.

To know who's the active player, the active player name is written in red with a dark background.

You have to fill this box by the right side to set your preferred total amassed point for a winner to be declared, or else it announces winner with any score greater than zero(0).
